                          Understanding Your Scores

                          Click any score card to learn what drives it, what strong performance looks like, and exactly how to improve.

                          Cybersecurity Score MFA · Backups · Security Training
                          ▾

                          Based on whether MFA is enabled, backups are in place, and staff have security training or policies.

                          📊 What factors make up this score?
                          • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) — Is MFA enabled on email, banking, cloud apps, and admin accounts? (+33 pts max)
                          • Backups — Are important files backed up and tested regularly? Manual/partial vs. automated counts. (+33 pts max)
                          • Security Training / Policy — Do you have a written policy or have staff received any security awareness training? (+34 pts max)
                          ✅ What does a strong score look like?
                          • MFA enabled on all critical accounts (email, banking, cloud tools)
                          • Automated, tested backups running at least weekly
                          • A written cybersecurity policy and at least basic staff training
                          • Score of 75%+ = Low cyber risk
                          ⚠️ Common gaps small businesses face
                          • Using shared passwords with no MFA — one breach exposes everything
                          • Backups exist but are never tested — you won't know they fail until you need them
                          • No written policy means staff can't follow rules that don't exist
                          • Free email accounts (Gmail/Yahoo) without MFA are the #1 attack vector for small businesses
                          🚀 How to improve your score — in order
                          1. Enable MFA on Google/Microsoft/banking today — free, takes 5 minutes
                          2. Set up automated backups to Google Drive, OneDrive, or an external drive
                          3. Use our Document Generator to create a free Cybersecurity Policy in one click
                          4. Share a 15-minute phishing awareness video with your team
                          Free CISA Tools ↗
                          AI Readiness Score AI Usage · Adoption Stage · Workflow Integration
                          ▾

                          Reflects your current level of AI tool adoption — from no usage through active integration into daily workflows.

                          📊 What factors make up this score?
                          • AI Usage — Are you actively using AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot)? None / Testing / Actively using. (+100 pts max)
                          • This score is intentionally focused — even testing AI tools puts you ahead of most small businesses.
                          ✅ What does a strong score look like?
                          • AI tools used weekly in at least one business workflow
                          • Examples: drafting emails, writing proposals, answering customer questions, summarizing notes
                          • Staff understand what AI can and can't do for the business
                          • An AI usage policy exists (even a one-pager)
                          💡 Quick AI wins for small businesses
                          • Customer follow-up emails — paste in a job description, ask AI to write a follow-up message
                          • Proposal/quote drafting — describe the project and let AI create a first draft
                          • Social media posts — give AI your offer details and ask for 5 post variations
                          • FAQ writing — ask AI to answer your 10 most common customer questions
                          • Policy documents — use our Document Generator to create policies instantly
                          🚀 How to improve your score — in order
                          1. Sign up for a free tier: Claude.ai or ChatGPT — both free
                          2. Pick ONE repetitive task this week and ask AI to do it for you
                          3. Generate an AI Usage Policy using our Document Generator
                          4. Reassess — selecting "Testing" on the next run will raise your score immediately
                          Try Claude Free ↗ Google AI Course ↗
                          Operational Score Digital Tools · Automation · Workflow Efficiency
                          ▾

                          Measures your use of digital tools and automation to run the business efficiently day-to-day.

                          📊 What factors make up this score?
                          • Digital Tools — Are you using mostly paper/manual processes, some digital tools, or fully integrated tools? (+50 pts max)
                          • Automation Level — Are repetitive tasks automated? None / Limited / Moderate or strong. (+50 pts max)
                          ✅ What does a strong score look like?
                          • Scheduling, invoicing, or communications use digital tools consistently
                          • At least one repetitive task is automated (reminders, follow-ups, reporting)
                          • Staff spend less time on manual data entry and more time serving customers
                          • Score of 75%+ = Strong operational foundation
                          ⚙️ High-impact automation for small businesses
                          • Scheduling — Calendly (free) eliminates phone-tag for appointments
                          • Invoicing — Wave (free) automates invoices, receipts, and reminders
                          • Customer follow-up — email sequences automatically follow up after a sale or inquiry
                          • Social media — schedule a week of posts in one sitting using Buffer or Meta Planner
                          • Accounting — connect your bank to QuickBooks and stop manual entry
                          🚀 How to improve your score — in order
                          1. List your top 3 most time-consuming manual tasks this week
                          2. Pick one and find a free digital tool that handles it (Calendly, Wave, or Google Forms)
                          3. Document the new workflow so staff can follow it consistently
                          4. Reassess — moving from "None" to "Limited" automation will lift your score by 25 pts
                          Calendly Free ↗ Wave Free ↗
                          Funding Readiness Business Docs · Online Presence · Growth Plan
                          ▾

                          Evaluates whether your business documents, online presence, and growth plan are ready for grants or funding applications.

                          📊 What factors make up this score?
                          • Business Documents — Are your registration docs, service descriptions, and financials organized? (+33 pts max)
                          • Online Presence — Do you have a website, Google Business Profile, or strong social presence? (+33 pts max)
                          • Growth Plan — Do you have documented goals, a roadmap, or a business plan? (+34 pts max)
                          ✅ What does funding-ready look like?
                          • Business registration, EIN, and any licenses are organized and accessible
                          • You can explain your business mission, customers, problem, and impact in writing in under 2 minutes
                          • A website or Google Business Profile exists and is up to date
                          • You have a written goal or plan for the next 12 months
                          • Score of 70%+ means you're ready to apply for most small business programs
                          💸 What funding is available right now?
                          • SBA Loan Programs — Low-interest loans up to $5M for eligible small businesses
                          • SCORE Mentors — Free business advisors who help with funding applications
                          • SBDC Centers — Free local help preparing grant and loan applications
                          • Local and state grants — many offer $2,500–$25,000 for small businesses in tech, food, and services
                          🚀 How to improve your score — in order
                          1. Create a one-page business summary (use our Business Profile Builder)
                          2. Set up a free Google Business Profile if you don't have a website
                          3. Write a simple 30/60/90-day goal list — even a half-page counts as a growth plan
                          4. Use our Grant Readiness Checklist to identify every missing document before applying
