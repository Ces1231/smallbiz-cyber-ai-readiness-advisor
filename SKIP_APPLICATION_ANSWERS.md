# Skip Application Answers

## What did you build?
I built an AI-powered readiness advisor that helps entrepreneurs and small business owners understand their cybersecurity posture, AI adoption readiness, operational gaps, and growth opportunities. The tool generates readiness scores, practical recommendations, ROI estimates, and a 30/60/90-day roadmap that business owners can act on immediately.

## What specific problem does this solve for business owners?
Many small business owners cannot afford dedicated cybersecurity, AI, or operations consultants, but they still need clear guidance to modernize, reduce risk, and grow. This tool turns complex technology and business-readiness questions into a simple self-service assessment with useful, actionable outputs.

## Anything else we should know?
The demo is designed to scale across restaurants, nonprofits, contractors, creators, consultants, online stores, retail shops, and service businesses. The long-term vision is to make readiness guidance more accessible to entrepreneurs through self-service assessments, industry-specific recommendations, and professional reporting.
