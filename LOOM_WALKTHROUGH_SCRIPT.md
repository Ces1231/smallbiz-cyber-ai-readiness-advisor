# 60–90 Second Loom Walkthrough Script

Hi, my name is Carnell Smith, founder of Champtron Systems LLC.

I built the SmallBiz Cyber & AI Readiness Advisor to help entrepreneurs and small business owners quickly understand where they stand with cybersecurity, AI adoption, and business growth readiness.

Many small businesses cannot afford dedicated consultants, cybersecurity staff, or AI experts. This tool gives them a simple guided assessment that turns complex technology decisions into clear scores, practical recommendations, and a 30/60/90-day roadmap.

In the demo, I can choose a sample business such as a restaurant, nonprofit, contractor, barber shop, or online store. The tool analyzes cybersecurity controls, AI usage, business documentation, online presence, and growth planning.

It then generates dashboard visuals, readiness scores, industry-specific recommendations, ROI estimates, risk-reduction guidance, and an executive report that can be printed or saved as a PDF.

The business impact is simple: small business owners get a practical roadmap they can act on immediately to reduce risk, improve efficiency, prepare for funding, and adopt AI in a responsible way.

This is built to scale across many types of entrepreneurs and small businesses, and I would be open to listing it on Skip or integrating it with Skip to help more business owners access these readiness tools.


## V4 Add-On Talking Point

This version also includes a Business Owner Action Center. After the readiness report is generated, the owner receives a priority checklist, estimated cost levels, tool recommendations, a document generator, monthly reassessment tracking, and a business risk register. This makes the product useful beyond a one-time assessment because the owner leaves with documents, actions, and a way to measure improvement over time.


## V5 Add-On Talking Point

The demo now includes a Funding Readiness and Submission Prep Mode. This does not compete with funding platforms. Instead, it helps small business owners prepare better business profiles, application answers, gap alerts, and readiness checklists before applying through platforms like Skip.
